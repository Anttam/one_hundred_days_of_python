My top 3 movies of all time

Ex Machina

Movie about a man not knowing if an AI robot is in love with him or if it's following it's protocol

Nightcrawler

Movie about an antisocial insomniac that makes a living recording footage for a news station and progressively displays an increasing lack of empathy for people

Inside Out

Movie about the emotions of a girl and how they react to the things she sees
